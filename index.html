Information Systems

Information Systems is an interdisciplinary field that combines computer science, business, and management to study how organizations use information technology to achieve their objectives.

Areas of Focus

Information Systems covers a wide range of topics, including system analysis and design, database management, network administration, cybersecurity, and IT project management.

Real-World Applications

Information Systems professionals design and implement technology solutions for various industries, such as finance, healthcare, education, and government, to improve efficiency, security, and decision-making.
